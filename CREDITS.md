# Credits

FJARCODE (FJAR) is built upon the work of many contributors and projects.

### Bitcoin Core
The foundation of this software is Bitcoin Core, developed by the Bitcoin Core developers.
- https://github.com/bitcoin/bitcoin
- https://bitcoincore.org

### Bitcoin Cash (BCH)
FJAR implements Bitcoin Cash consensus rules, including ASERT DAA, 32MB blocks, and CashAddr.
- https://bitcoincash.org
- https://gitlab.com/bitcoin-cash-node/bitcoin-cash-node
